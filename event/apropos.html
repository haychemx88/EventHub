About EventHub

Discover our mission, our team, and our passion for connecting people through unforgettable events.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, EventHub was born from four friends' passion for cultural events and the desire to make event discovery and organization more accessible to everyone.

What started as a small local project in Tunis quickly grew into a national platform, connecting thousands of people to events that matter to them.

Today, EventHub is the leading platform for discovering and organizing events of all sizes, from small community meetups to major cultural festivals.

Our Mission

At EventHub, we believe live events have the power to transform lives, create communities, and bring people together.

Our mission is to democratize access to cultural events and provide organizers with the tools they need to succeed.

We are committed to:

  • Making event discovery intuitive and personalized
  • Making event organization accessible to all
  • Creating meaningful connections between attendees
  • Supporting creators and local small businesses
